Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Effective: January 1, 2025

Your horses' health records, coggins certificates, and personal information are yours. We do not sell your data to third parties. Ever.

Who we are

HorseCamp ("we," "us," or "our") is an equestrian travel application operated by HorseCamp, accessible at HorseCampFinder.com and through our iOS and Android mobile applications. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@horsecampfinder.com.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information we collect automatically when you use the app, and information from third-party sources.

Information you provide:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password
  • Profile information: home base location, rig type and length, hookup preferences
  • Horse information: horse names, breeds, dates of birth, and registration numbers
  • Health documents: uploaded coggins certificates, health certificates (CVI), brand inspection certificates, and vaccination records (stored as images or PDFs)
  • Trip data: planned itineraries, saved camps, and travel dates
  • Reviews and submissions: camp reviews, ratings, and community-submitted camp information
  • Payment information: processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play — we do not store your payment card details

Information collected automatically:

  • Location data: approximate or precise location when you use the map or camp search features (only when you grant permission)
  • Device information: device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: features used, searches performed, camps viewed, and app session information
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

How we use your information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the HorseCamp app and services
  • To power camp search, filtering by rig size and hookup type, and trip planning
  • To send coggins expiration reminders, health certificate alerts, and trip notifications you have enabled
  • To surface nearby vets, farriers, and emergency equine clinics based on your location
  • To generate your QR check-in packet for camp arrival
  • To respond to your support requests and communicate with you
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

Your horse health records

Documents you upload (coggins, health certs, brand inspection certificates, vaccination records) are stored securely and encrypted. They are:

  • Accessible only to you and, when you explicitly share them, to the camp or party you choose to share with via QR code or link
  • Never sold, shared with advertisers, or disclosed to third parties without your explicit action
  • Deleted from our servers within 30 days if you delete them from your account or close your account

Location data

We request location permission to show camps near you and to power route planning. You can deny location permission and still use the app by searching manually. We do not share your precise location history with third parties. Location data is used only to provide the map and search features.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate the app (cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting) — bound by data processing agreements
  • Mapping services: We use Mapbox for maps — your location may be transmitted to Mapbox when you use map features, subject to Mapbox's Privacy Policy
  • Weather and hazard services: We use public APIs (National Weather Service, USFS) to display route alerts — no personal information is shared
  • Law enforcement: Only if required by valid legal process
  • Business transfers: If HorseCamp is acquired or merges with another company, your data may transfer subject to the same privacy protections

Data retention

We retain your account information and horse records for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Aggregated, anonymized data (such as camp search trends) may be retained indefinitely.

Your rights and choices

  • Access: You can access and download your data from within the app
  • Correction: You can update your profile, horse records, and documents at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual documents, horse profiles, or your entire account from the app settings
  • Notifications: You can enable or disable all push notifications from the app settings
  • Location: You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings

California residents may have additional rights under CCPA. Virginia, Colorado, and other state residents may have rights under applicable state privacy laws. Contact us at support@horsecampfinder.com to exercise your rights.

Children's privacy

HorseCamp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

Third-party links

The app may contain links to camp websites, vet offices, and other third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or sending an email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
