Everything you need on the road

Built for haulers, not just campers.

Find camps that fit your rig

Filter by trailer length, hookup type (30A or 50A), stalls, paddocks, and sewer. A 65ft gooseneck gets different results than a bumper pull — the way it should be.

Plan your whole trip

Build multi-stop itineraries across all 50 states. Automatic seasonal availability check flags closed camps before you commit. Weather, wildfire, and flood alerts along your exact route.

Store your horse's papers

Upload coggins certificates, health certs, brand inspection docs, and vaccines. Stored only on your device — never uploaded to any server. Automatic expiration reminders. Share a QR check-in packet at camp arrival.

Real reviews from haulers

Reviews from people who showed up with 3 horses and a gooseneck — not RV campers. Site-specific notes like "Site 7 fits 65ft" and "paddock auto-waterers in stalls 3–6 only."

Vets and farriers on the road

Large animal vets, farriers accepting new clients, and 24-hour emergency equine clinics mapped near your location and along your route. Because emergencies don't wait.

Wildfire, storm & flood alerts

Active hazard overlays on your route map. Know about road closures, fire watch zones, tornado watches, and flood-prone crossings before you hook up the trailer.

Getting started

On the trail in minutes.

Set up your rig

Tell us your trailer type, length, and hookup needs. We filter every result to match your setup — gooseneck or bumper pull, 30A or 50A, bumper to bumper.

Add your horses

Upload coggins, health certs, brand inspection, and vaccines for each horse. Photo your paper certificate — no scanner needed. We track expiration dates automatically.

Search & plan

Find camps near you or plan a full cross-country route. Seasonal availability, weather hazards, and state brand inspection requirements are checked automatically.

Ride with confidence

Share your QR check-in packet at the gate. Get real-time alerts along the way. Find a vet if you need one. HorseCamp has you covered from first stop to last.
